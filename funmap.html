The project involves developing an interactive prototype for an App. FunMap is designed to help parents effortlessly discover family-friendly and child-oriented places. It includes a wide range of destinations, such as playgrounds, libraries, creative labs, and activities. At the same time, it provides essential details like age group suitability, accessibility features, parking availability, and more, making it quicker and easier for parents to plan outings and ensure their children have a fun and enjoyable experience.
